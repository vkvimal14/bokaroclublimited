Welcome to Bokaro Club

Step into a realm of luxury and excitement at Bokaro Club Limited. Indulge in a culinary journey at our exquisite restaurant, sip on expertly crafted cocktails at the vibrant bar, and dive into a world of indoor sports and leisure activities.

Unwind by the sparkling swimming pool, challenge your friends to a game of badminton on our courts, and host unforgettable events in our elegant event halls. Bokaro Club is where unforgettable memories are made!

Join our vibrant community and immerse yourself in a lifestyle of sophistication and fun. Whether you seek relaxation or adventure, Bokaro Club offers the perfect blend of leisure and excitement for all its members.

Aquatic Bliss

Dive into refreshing waters and experience a world of aquatic relaxation at our state-of-the-art swimming pool. Whether you're looking to swim laps, enjoy a leisurely dip, or simply soak up the sun, our pristine pool offers the perfect escape. With crystal-clear water, comfortable loungers, and attentive staff, you'll find yourself immersed in a tranquil oasis that invigorates both body and mind.

Memorable Occasions

Create unforgettable moments in our versatile event halls, perfect for hosting weddings, conferences, and special celebrations. Our exceptional spaces, combined with our dedicated event planning services, ensure that every occasion is transformed into a truly remarkable experience.

The Steelmakers' Lounge

Unwind and recharge at The Steelmakers' Lounge, Bokaro Club's exclusive bar dedicated to the hardworking employees of Bokaro Steel Plant. After a long day of shaping steel and driving progress, this is your haven to relax and connect with fellow steelmakers. Indulge in a carefully curated selection of premium spirits, ice-cold beers, and expertly crafted cocktails, each designed to satisfy your discerning taste.

Frequently Asked Questions

What amenities does Bokaro Club offer?

Bokaro Club offers a range of amenities including a Restorant, Bar, indoor sports facilities, swimming pool, badminton court, and event halls.

Is membership required to access the facilities?

Yes, membership is required to enjoy the exclusive privileges and services at Bokaro Club.

Can I host events at Bokaro Club?

Absolutely! Our event halls are perfect for hosting memorable occasions and celebrations.

Are there any age restrictions for membership?

Membership at Bokaro Club is open to individuals of all ages who appreciate luxury and entertainment.
